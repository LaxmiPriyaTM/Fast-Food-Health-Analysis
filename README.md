# Analysis of Fast Food Consumption and Its Impact on Health

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the impact of fast food consumption and lifestyle habits on health indicators such as Body Mass Index (BMI), energy levels, and overall health score using Data Science techniques.

The goal is to understand how daily habits like diet, sleep, and physical activity influence health outcomes.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze the relationship between fast food consumption and BMI  
- Study the impact of calorie intake on body health  
- Understand how physical activity and sleep affect overall health  
- Identify patterns using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)  

---

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset contains information about individuals' lifestyle and health factors, including:

- Age  
- Gender  
- Fast Food Meals Per Week  
- Average Daily Calories  
- BMI  
- Physical Activity Hours Per Week  
- Sleep Hours Per Day  
- Energy Level Score  
- Digestive Issues  
- Doctor Visits Per Year  
- Overall Health Score  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Python   
- Pandas  
- Matplotlib  
- Seaborn  

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed
- Fast Food Consumption vs BMI  
- Daily Calories vs BMI  
- Physical Activity vs Health Score  
- Sleep Hours vs Energy Level  
- Correlation Heatmap  

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Increased fast food consumption is associated with higher BMI  
- Higher calorie intake contributes to weight gain  
- Regular physical activity improves overall health  
- Adequate sleep enhances energy levels  
- Lifestyle factors significantly influence health outcomes  

---

## 📈 Visualizations
The project includes:
- Scatter plots to identify relationships  
- Correlation heatmap to understand variable dependencies  

---

## 🚀 Conclusion
This analysis shows that lifestyle choices such as diet, exercise, and sleep play a crucial role in determining health outcomes. Data-driven insights can help individuals adopt healthier habits.
