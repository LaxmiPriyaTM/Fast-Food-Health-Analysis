# Analysis of Fast Food Consumption and Its Impact on Health

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the impact of fast food consumption and lifestyle habits on health indicators such as BMI, energy level, and overall health score using Data Science techniques.

## 🎯 Objective
Understand how fast food consumption affects BMI
Analyze the impact of physical activity and sleep on health
Identify patterns using exploratory data analysis
## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
Python
Pandas
Matplotlib
Seaborn
## 📊 Analysis Performed
Fast Food Meals vs BMI
Daily Calories vs BMI
Physical Activity vs Health Score
Sleep Hours vs Energy Level
Correlation Heatmap
## 💡 Key Insights
Increased fast food consumption is associated with higher BMI
Higher physical activity leads to better health scores
Adequate sleep improves energy levels
## 📂 Dataset

The dataset includes features like:

Fast food consumption
Calories intake
BMI
Sleep hours
Physical activity
## 🚀 Conclusion

Lifestyle factors such as diet, exercise, and sleep significantly influence overall health. Data analysis helps in understanding these relationships and promoting healthier habits.
